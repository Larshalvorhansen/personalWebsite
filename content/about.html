Hi, welcome to my personal website!

I like to apply the principles of engineering and economics for solving real-world problems.

From a young age, I've been fascinated by how things work and the mechanics behind everyday objects. This curiosity led me to pursue a career in both engineering and socioeconomics. Ive been lucky to allready have worked as a summern at Firda and SONY.

Recently I have transitioned from my position as a Systems Engineer at Orbit NTNU to focus more on my new project: The Lambda Simulation Project. This is indeed very exiting, yes.

I'm a full-time student at NTNU in Trondheim, pursuing both a Master's in Electronic Engineering and a Bachelor's in Economics. hobby projects often involve tinkering with new technologies and applying them in creative ways. I enjoy collaborating with others and learning from different perspectives to drive impactful results.

When I'm not studying or working professionally, you might find me outside riding a road bike, reading something nerdy, or playing the bass guitar or doing some random hobby project hobby pro. If not, I might be making some more digital art :)
